About Me

I am graduate student in the Computer Science Department at George Mason University, advised by Prof. Xuesu Xiao. I graduated from Miami University with a bachelor degree in Computer Science in 2022. My research interest lies in task and motion planning for service robots.

Besides research, I am also interested in Large-Language Model (LLM), Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF), and how to create efficient Machine Learning Operations (MLOps) to empower ML applications with better data.
